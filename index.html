Turning Design Into Reality

My name is Carl Lombardi. I am an aspiring front-end web developer with full-stack experience. My goal is to bring an unparralled work ethic to any position and create beautiful, simple user interfaces for clients.

Design Philosophy

When it comes to my personal design philosophy, my main goal is to "do less" with more. I believe the best websites feature a clean UI which allows even non-technical people to be comfortable and conduct business.

Technology Skills

I have recently graduated from Rutgers Full Stack Development Bootcamp and in the process have gained experience with HTML/CSS/Javascript and React on the front end as well as SQL and non-SQL databases on the back end.

People Skills

Beyond the hard technology skills and design philosophy developed during Bootcamp, my general approach is people first. Whether I am working as part of a team in a business or on my own, I believe in bringing a postive, hard-working attitude to any job or task.
