Welcome to my portfolio website. My name is Carl Lombardi and I am an aspiring full-stack developer, aiming to bring a fresh perspective to the industry. I began my career in the insurance field, working over nine years at American International Group in the Excess Casualty Claims department. After departing from AIG, I have decided to make a career change into development.

I have recently graduated from Rutgers Full Stack Development Bootcamp and am ready for my next challenge. I am a 2009 graduate of Fairfield University in Fairfield, CT, with a B.A. in English/Journalism. I spent the summers of 2005 and 2006 as a play-by-play and color commentator for the Bridgeport Bluefish of the Atlantic League of Professional Baseball on WVOF. In 2006, I was a member of the broadcast team for the 2006 ALPB All Star Game from the Ballpark at Harbor Yard in Bridgeport, CT
